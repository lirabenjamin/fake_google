Position: Medical Scribe
Relieve the physician of clerical duties, allowing the physician to focus directly on clinical
care. A scribe’s role is limited to documentation and efficiency management.
The scribe observes the physician during patient encounters and performs documentation on the
physician’s behalf. Under the physician’s direction, they enter information into the patient’s
electronic or written chart.
Scribes function as the physician’s assistant and requires access to electronic databases. Within the
framework of an Electronic Medical Record (EMR) system the scribe needs the functionality to find
old records and results. The scribe needs the ability to enter data into the chart on the
physician’s behalf.

Scribe position. My organizational and technological ability to accurately record data in a timely manner aligns
with the position requirements to accurately chart medical information to enhance patient care. With
previous extracurricular experience as the Secretary of the MedCats student organization and academic

During my freshmen year of college, I acted as Secretary of the MedCats organization and documented

document from group discussions. I became efficient at documenting meetings with attention to detail,
summarizing key points, and organizing the notes in a way that someone who didn’t attend the meeting could
easily remain informed. I am confident I could apply these same documentation skills to enter accurate data in
the EMR system.
I am passionate about gaining experience to apply my academic background in Physiology to support the
efficiency of medical appointments for both physicians and patients. Last fall, I took a course in Medical
Terminology where we learned about root terms and word associations to build a foundation for
understanding complex medical terminology. My academic background and understanding of terminology will
support my ability to find old records and results.
With my growing knowledge in Physiology and my skills in communication, organization, and documentation, I
would be a great fit for Scribe America. Thank you for your consideration and I look forward to hearing from
you!
