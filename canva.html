Printable cover letters by Canva

Cover letters are essential during the recruitment process. It’s an effective way of introducing yourself, your skills, and why you are interested in a specific company. Only after reading the cover letter will the recruiter go through your long list of achievements and career milestones before considering you for an interview.

Canva’s free cover letter templates can help you make a good first impression. Start by finding a cover letter design that best suits your personality and the industry you’re in. Look professional even in a creative agency, or stand out in a formal corporate setting. Our editable cover letters include short samples, so you won’t have to type everything from scratch.

Once you’ve found an idea of how your cover letter will look like, select your template and begin personalizing it. Draft your letter straight from the dashboard, or paste it from your notes. You won’t need to settle with typical typography because we have many standard but pleasing fonts to choose from. Add in an extra pop by creating a letterhead with your name. Explore our shape and line tools, which you can fill with a basic color. Don’t forget to include your contact information in the placeholders provided!

When you’ve finished editing, download your free cover letter as a PDF file or export it in a shareable link or high-resolution image format. Don’t worry—your file remains in your Canva account, so you can always create a new cover letter from your template. Make multiple copies to customize for every company you apply to. Your cover letter is formatted to standard letter size, so you can easily print and attach it to your existing resumé.

Your dream job interview is an arm’s length away. All that’s left is to impress your future employer with that killer resumé and cover letter, created through Canva’s templates.
