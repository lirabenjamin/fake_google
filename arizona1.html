Position Summary
The BIO5 Institute is currently seeking highly motivated candidates for Student Assistant positions to
handle a wide array of administration functions including reception; coordination of resource
reservations and upkeep for audio-visual equipment and golf carts; and courier services. The selected
candidate will play an important role in the efficient and professional operation of the
administrative suite.
The Office Assistant must be flexible and feel comfortable asking visitors for identification and
purpose of visit to determine or obtain authorization to enter area.
Duties and responsibilities will include the following:
• Greets building visitors at front desk and informs building residents of their guests’ arrival.
• Answers and screens calls, providing general information, taking messages and directing calls
as appropriate.
• Thorough understanding of building access rules, regulations and policies and the ability to
convey and enforce them.
• Proficient use of the calendaring system to initiate appointments or reservations and refer to
information provided by the calendar.
• Primary contact for room & resource reservations.
• Deliver messages, documents, packages and other items to individuals or groups within the
building or on campus as needed.
• Receiving, unpacking, verifying, recording and storing incoming merchandise or material
appropriately according to.
• Arranging for the delivery or pick up of products for various groups (labs) in the building.
Plus, preparing items for shipments including; assembling, addressing, stamping/labeling, and
shipping merchandise or material according to policies, rules and regulations.
• Escort visiting VIPs, as needed.
• Sorting and distributing mail.
• Perform opening and closing processes/procedures as needed.
• Maintains an organized and clean office suite

Dear Hiring Manager,
I am highly motivated freshman Environmental Science major and I am excited to apply for the
Administration Desk Student Assistant at BIO5 Institute. As someone with interest in many areas
of research, BIO5’s interdisciplinary approach is inspiring, and I would love to play a part in
making students and guests feel welcome there. I would love the opportunity to apply my ability
to learn policy and procedures quickly and my experience as an office aide to the Student
Assistant position at BIO5.
During my senior year of high school, I was selected to be an office aide during one of my free
periods. In that role I welcomed visitors, answered the main office phone, and organized the daily
incoming and outgoing mail. I viewed staff Calendars in Outlook to verify their appointment times
and schedule meetings as necessary. If there were any one-time projects or special events, I was
often put in charge because of my ability to take initiative and work independently when given
general instructions.
I would feel comfortable greeting visitors and directing them to where they need to go. I have an
outgoing personality and ability to learn new tasks quickly, so I would have no problem
understanding building access rules to ensure the safety and security of the building.
My experience coordinating multiple administrative services while maintaining excellent
customer service make me a strong candidate for the Student Assistant position. Thank you for
taking the time to review my application and I look forward to hearing from you.
Sincerely,
Quinn Rivera
