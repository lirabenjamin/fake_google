STDT 2 - Global Study Program Student Assistant

Student Assistant:

As a Global Study Program Student Assistant, you will assist incoming international students at UC Davis through the organization of social and academic events, general advising, and through general administrative support to the Global Study Program staff. This is an opportunity to further develop your office and organization skills, gain experience working with international students, and work in a supportive and professional environment.

The Global Study Program Student Assistant is vital in ensuring visiting international students receive timely support and assistance through administrative processes occurring at all phases of their study at UC Davis. You will also help in the coordination of events/services for students and/or faculty.

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Plan and participate in Global Study Program orientation, cultural activities, and social events.
  • Under the direct supervision of University Programs staff, assist with aspects of student recruitment, applications, and marketing procedures for various programs.
  • Utilize GSP social media platforms to market events, engage with students, and collect photos
  • Complete special projects as assigned.
  • Provide support to international students through advising and referrals to campus resources.

Requirements:

  • Able to begin training Spring 2024 and continue throughout the 2024/2025 school year
  • Enthusiasm for working with international students.
  • Demonstrated ability to provide excellent customer service to a variety of clients
  • Demonstrated skill and experience with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Teams, PowerPoint etc.)
  • Demonstrated analytical skills to exercise independent judgment and problem solving
  • Interpersonal skills to work effectively with a diverse and dynamic professional staff and students
  • Ability to work 5 to 12 hours per week including occasional overtime, evenings, and weekends
  • Basic skills with software and social media platforms such as Canvas, Instagram, and Twitter
  • Ability to lift and move boxes of up to 40 pounds.
  • Video editing and digital media design skills preferred but not required
