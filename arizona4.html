ANALYST POSITION
Job Summary
Fontana is currently accepting applications for professional Analyst positions. Entry-level
and early-career professionals are encouraged to apply. Job duties may include:
Organizing, structuring, and manipulating large data sets.
Using high-level proficiency in Excel to integrate, analyze, and display data
table or chart form.
Database programming in SQL and other languages to manage and
manipulate large data sets that are received in various formats.
GIS Data analysis, manipulation, and, and display.
Statistical and econometric analysis and programming.
Financial data and statement analysis.
Job Qualifications
The ideal candidate would have:
Bachelors or masters degree establishing appropriate analytical and skills
proficiency.
Strong general mathematical acumen and critical thinking skills (this is far
more important than mastery of advanced numerical techniques).
Proficiency in a programming environment.
A strong ability to communicate verbally and in writing.
Strong abstract and systematic reasoning skills.
Commitment to respond to a deadline-sensitive environment.
A sincere desire to master the learning curve inherent in our industry.

Dear Hiring Manager,
As a recent graduate with a degree in Applied Mathematics, I am excited to apply to the
Analyst position within Fontana Group, Inc. I was particularly motivated to apply upon reading
the firms emphasis in hiring someone with strong mathematical acumen and critical thinking
skills, which was the focus of my interdisciplinary degree program and two of my greatest
strengths. I have always had a passion for using mathematical analysis to solve problems and am
a self-motivated, organized individual who can commit to deadlines and communicate
effectively with others. My academic background in mathematics, research experience, and
professional experience collaborating in professional settings would make me a strong candidate
for the Analyst position.
Over the past two years of my undergraduate career, I conducted and participated in
multiple research projects in the fields of biological physics and atmospheric sciences. Through a
combination of applied mathematics coursework, physics coursework, and these research
projects, I was able to use mathematical analysis to solve a multitude of different problems,
which strengthened my critical thinking and solidified my mathematical acumen. My greatest
strengths are my ability to analyze statistics and write programs using Python, but I also have
extensive experience using partial differential equations to create mathematical models. These
skills would allow me to make an immediate impact at Fontana, especially in a deadline-
sensitive consulting environment.
In addition to technical experience, I am skilled at presenting and communicating
information and working in a collaborative environment. I have experience presenting research,
providing consistent updates to advisors, and working as a part of a team. I pride myself on
having written and oral communication skills that are as sharp as my technical skills. This past
year, I was promoted from a Math Lab teaching coordinator to a teaching assistant, where I not
only facilitated classes of 30-50 students, but also led groups of coordinators to adapt to online
learning environments and maximize learning efficiency.
My strong math and problem-solving skills and desire to continue to learn within a new
industry make me a strong fit for the Analyst role. I would love to hear from you to learn more
about the position, the skills you value, and the role that I could play as an analyst for The
Fontana Group in an interview. Thank you for your time and consideration.
Sincerely,
Jessica Murray
